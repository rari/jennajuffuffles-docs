# FAQ

_Last updated:_ 2026-06-10\
_Applies to Stardew Valley:_ 1.6.15+

Quick answers about the current JennaJuffuffles collection lineup. For **installation and Vortex**, start at [New Player Install Instructions](../Installation/index.md). For **errors and weird behavior**, use [Troubleshooting & FAQ](../Troubleshooting/index.md) and your [SMAPI log](../Troubleshooting/your-smapi-log.md).

## Collections & Nexus

- **Is Nexus Premium required?** No. Nexus Premium is not required.
- **Where are these collections available?** These collections are only available on Nexus.
- **Why Vortex Collections?** Vortex Collections is used because it provides the collection creator the tools to create a curated experience, and Nexus largely because it has active moderation and pays mod creators. These were day zero priorities!
- **When should mods be updated?** For best results, update mods only when the related collection updates.
- **What should I do if I crash while loading?** Players who experience slow loading times, including on new game start, avoid alt-tabbing or clicking away as this can cause a crash.
- **Can I get a zip of all files?** No. Please do not ask how to pirate mods included in these collections.
- **Can I clone bundled content in public collections?** No. Redistribution of bundled content is not permitted. Some files are original to these collections, and others belong to third-party creators and are included under their terms. Personal use is fine.
